This is My Life

My name is Victor Ronnuel David. I am 22 years old and was born on January 30, 2003, in Pusok, Cemento, Lapu-Lapu City. Since childhood, I have loved repairing things and experimenting with DC motors, batteries, and speakers.

As I grew up, I developed my skills and became a cellphone and laptop technician. Being a technician is very challenging because even a small mistake can lead to big problems. However, that is part of the job. One of the things I love about this profession is that I can earn ₱600 to ₱1,000+ in just a minute, which is really rewarding! Hahaha!

Due to a lack of customers in my repair business, I decided to work part-time as a bartender at Uncle Brew. Even though the salary is only ₱63 per hour, the work is not as difficult compared to my previous job in fast food.

From 2017 to 2023, I also worked in fast food as a part-timer. Currently, I work from Monday to Saturday for 5 hours, earning ₱315 per day. On Sundays, I work 8 hours and earn ₱501.

I work hard because I want to earn more money and secure my future. Now, I have a Cebuana Bank account where I deposit my salary. I also set aside some of my earnings for my daily expenses and education.

This is my life, and I am proud of my journey so far.
